Ashley J. Williams

AKA El Jefe

The warrior foretold through the ancient writings connected to the Necronomicon Ex Mortis.

The Reluctant Hero

Ashley Joanna Williams began his journey as an ordinary man with no knowledge of ancient evil, forgotten prophecies or supernatural forces. A trip to a remote cabin in the woods would transform his life forever and place him at the centre of a battle against the Evil Dead.

Ash is not a hero who seeks adventure or glory. He is a survivor who is repeatedly forced to stand against forces far beyond human understanding. Through loss, suffering and determination, he would eventually become the legendary warrior known as El Jefe.

His destiny begins with the discovery of the Necronomicon Ex Mortis and the awakening of an ancient evil hidden within the Knowby cabin.

The Beginning Of Ash's Journey

The Evil Dead (1981)

Ash and his friends travel towards the isolated Knowby cabin.

The Journey To The Woods

Before becoming the legendary Deadite fighter, Ash Williams was simply travelling with his girlfriend Linda and his friends Cheryl, Scotty and Shelly to a remote cabin hidden deep within the Tennessee woods.

The group expected a peaceful getaway, unaware that the cabin had a dark history. Years earlier, Professor Raymond Knowby had discovered the ancient Necronomicon Ex Mortis while studying forgotten languages and supernatural forces.

Inside the cabin, Ash and his friends discovered Knowby's recordings. When the passages from the Book of the Dead were played aloud, an ancient Kandarian force was awakened.

The Necronomicon Ex Mortis awakens the forces of darkness.

The Awakening Of The Kandarian Evil

The evil unleashed from the Necronomicon was not simply a creature, but an ancient supernatural force capable of possessing the living and transforming them into Deadites.

The cabin quickly became a place of terror as Ash watched the people around him fall victim to the darkness. Cheryl, Shelly and Scotty were taken by the evil, leaving Ash isolated and fighting for his survival.

The man who entered the woods looking for a weekend away was forced into a battle against an enemy he could never have imagined.

Ash is forced to face the Deadites, including the person he loved most.

The Loss Of Linda

As the Kandarian evil spreads throughout the cabin, Ash suffers his greatest personal loss. Linda, the woman he loves, becomes one of the victims of the darkness unleashed by the Necronomicon.

After being possessed, Linda returns as a Deadite, forcing Ash into a battle against someone who was once his closest companion. The evil does not simply attack Ash physically; it uses his memories and emotions against him.

Ash is eventually forced to destroy the Deadite that Linda has become. This moment changes him forever. The ordinary man who arrived at the cabin is gone, replaced by someone who has been forced to fight against the impossible.

Ash destroys the Necronomicon, believing the nightmare has ended.

The Destruction Of The Necronomicon

With the cabin consumed by darkness and his friends lost, Ash discovers that the only way to end the nightmare is to destroy the source of the evil itself.

He throws the Necronomicon Ex Mortis into the fire, believing that the Book of the Dead has finally been defeated. Ash has survived the horrors of the cabin and appears to have ended the nightmare that began with the discovery of Professor Knowby's recordings.

However, the evil unleashed from the book is not so easily defeated. Although the physical Necronomicon is destroyed, the Kandarian force that was awakened remains active.

As Ash stands alone outside the cabin, the unseen force rushes toward him, beginning the next chapter of his battle against the Evil Dead.

The events of Evil Dead 2 continue directly from Ash's final moments in the first film.

The Evil Dead Continue

The beginning of Evil Dead 2 serves as a brief retelling of Ash's first encounter with the Evil Dead. The recap brings the story back to the moment where Ash destroyed the Necronomicon and the Kandarian force attacked him.

The story then continues immediately from that point. Ash is thrown through the woods by the supernatural force and becomes the next victim of the evil he believed he had escaped.

The original Necronomicon Ex Mortis has already been destroyed. The battle Ash faces now is not the discovery of a new book, but the continuation of the same evil that was unleashed during his first encounter.

Ash begins to discover that the darkness surrounding the cabin is far older and more powerful than he understood.

Ash's Possession

After being attacked by the Kandarian force, Ash is temporarily possessed by the evil surrounding the cabin. The same darkness that claimed his friends attempts to consume him as well.

Unlike those who fell before him, Ash is able to resist the possession and eventually returns to himself. This becomes one of the first signs that Ash is different from ordinary people who encounter the Deadites.

Ash awakens alone, once again trapped at the cabin and forced to confront the supernatural forces that refuse to let him go.

The survivor of the first nightmare is beginning to become something more. Every battle, every loss and every encounter with the Deadites pushes Ash closer to becoming the warrior he was destined to become.

The evil continues to torment Ash at the cabin.

The Return Of The Deadites

The Kandarian evil continues its assault on Ash, using the cabin and its history as a source of terror. The nightmare that began during his first visit is far from over.

The darkness attempts to break Ash mentally as well as physically, forcing him to relive the horrors that changed his life forever.

But unlike the frightened man who first entered the cabin, Ash now begins to fight back. His survival instinct develops into something greater: the determination of a warrior.

Linda returns as a Deadite, forcing Ash to face the past he thought he had escaped.

The Return Of Linda

The evil surrounding the cabin continues to attack Ash by bringing back the memories and horrors of his first encounter. Linda, who Ash lost during the original nightmare, returns once again as a Deadite.

Her return is one of the cruelest attacks against Ash. The Kandarian force uses the image of someone he loved to torment him, reminding him that the darkness can corrupt even the people closest to him.

The possessed Linda becomes one of Ash's most unforgettable battles. The horror reaches its peak when her body continues moving after death, creating the bizarre and terrifying headless dance that becomes one of the most iconic moments of his fight against the Evil Dead.

Ash is forced to accept that the past cannot be recovered. The only way forward is to continue fighting the evil that has taken everything from him.

Ash transforms his greatest loss into his most famous weapon.

The Birth Of The Chainsaw Hand

The battle against the Evil Dead takes another devastating turn when Ash's own hand becomes possessed by the Kandarian evil.

The possessed hand attacks Ash, leaving him with no choice but to remove it before the darkness can consume him completely. What begins as a desperate act of survival becomes the moment that creates his legendary weapon.

Ash replaces his lost hand with a chainsaw, turning a painful reminder of his suffering into a symbol of defiance against the Deadites.

This is the moment where Ash truly begins to change. He is no longer simply trying to survive the Evil Dead — he is becoming the person capable of fighting them.

The secrets surrounding the Necronomicon continue through the missing pages.

Annie Knowby And The Missing Pages

Ash's battle changes when Annie Knowby arrives at the cabin. The daughter of Professor Raymond Knowby, Annie brings her father's research and the missing pages connected to the Necronomicon Ex Mortis.

The original Book of the Dead was destroyed by Ash at the end of his first encounter. The pages Annie carries are not a replacement for the book, but surviving sections containing knowledge about the ancient forces connected to it.

Through Annie's discoveries, Ash learns that the evil surrounding the cabin is part of something much older and more powerful than he originally understood.

The battle is no longer just about escaping the woods. Ash is becoming part of an ancient conflict between humanity and the supernatural forces of darkness.

The destiny of the hero from the sky begins to reveal itself.

The Hero From The Sky

The knowledge contained within the missing pages reveals that Ash's journey is connected to an ancient prophecy. The writings describe a hero who will arrive from the sky and stand against the forces of evil.

Ash does not see himself as a chosen warrior. He is still the same reluctant man who wanted nothing more than to escape the nightmare. However, every battle has proven that he possesses the strength and determination needed to oppose the darkness.

The Deadites destroyed his old life, but they also forged him into the warrior the prophecy described.

Ash is taken beyond his own time and toward his ultimate destiny.

The Time Vortex

After the battle at the cabin, the forces connected to the Necronomicon open a powerful portal. Ash is pulled through the vortex and taken away from his own time.

The man who entered the woods searching for a simple getaway is transported into a forgotten age of kingdoms, warriors and ancient evil.

Ash discovers that his battle with the Evil Dead was never only about the cabin. The darkness stretches across history, and his role in the fight against it is far greater than he ever imagined.

The Legend Of El Jefe

Ash Williams' journey is the transformation of an ordinary man into a legendary warrior. He never asked for the responsibility placed upon him, but every encounter with the Evil Dead pushed him closer to fulfilling his destiny.

From the Knowby cabin to ancient kingdoms, Ash became the one person capable of standing against the Deadites. His courage, stubbornness and refusal to surrender made him the enemy that the forces of darkness feared most.

The reluctant survivor became the hero from the sky. The man with the chainsaw hand became the legend known as El Jefe.

Wherever the Evil Dead rise, Ash Williams will be there to face them.
