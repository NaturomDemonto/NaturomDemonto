Ashley J. Williams

AKA El Jefe

The prophesied hero as illustrated within the ancient Necronomicon Ex Mortis.

Ash

Ashley Joanna Williams is the central protagonist of the Evil Dead franchise and the most famous opponent of the Necronomicon Ex Mortis. Once an ordinary young man, Ash's life was forever changed after a weekend trip to a remote cabin unleashed the ancient forces of the Book of the Dead. Over the years, he would battle Deadites across different eras, becoming a reluctant hero whose courage, determination and resilience earned him legendary status in humanity's war against the forces of evil. Unknown to Ash for many years, his destiny had already been written within the ancient prophecies of the Necronomicon itself, foretelling the arrival of the prophesied hero from the sky who would one day stand against the darkness and fight to destroy the Evil Dead.

The Evil Dead (1981)

The Journey To The Knowby Cabin

Before becoming the legendary warrior known as El Jefe, Ashley Joanna Williams was simply a young man travelling with his friends to a remote cabin in the woods of Tennessee. Ash arrived at the cabin alongside Linda, Cheryl, Scotty and Shelly, unaware that the location held the remains of Professor Raymond Knowby's forbidden research into the Necronomicon Ex Mortis.

The discovery of the Book of the Dead and the recordings left behind by Professor Knowby would change Ash's life forever. When the ancient writings were spoken aloud, a powerful evil force was awakened, beginning Ash's lifelong battle against the forces of darkness.

The Awakening Of The Kandarian Evil

The reading of the Necronomicon unleashed an ancient entity that began possessing the living and the dead. Ash soon found himself trapped in the cabin with his friends as one by one they fell under the influence of the evil force.
