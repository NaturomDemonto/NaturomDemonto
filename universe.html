The Evil Dead Universe

The Necronomicon And The Forces Of Evil

The Evil Dead universe is connected by one ancient source of evil: the Necronomicon Ex Mortis, the Book of the Dead. Throughout history, different copies of this forbidden text have been discovered. Each book contains its own secrets, rituals and deadly passages that can unleash the forces of darkness upon the world.

This archive documents the known Necronomicons, their history, and the stories connected to each version of the Book of the Dead.

Necronomicon Book I

(The Face Book)

The original known Necronomicon Ex Mortis. This is the ancient Sumerian book discovered in the woods of Tennessee and the one responsible for Ash Williams' lifelong battle against the Deadites. This book's entity is Kandar, and its deadites are alin to spiteful children enjoying pulling the wings of a fly, with a demented sense of humour.

This book is connected to the events of The Evil Dead, Evil Dead II, Army Of Darkness and Ash vs Evil Dead.

Necronomicon Book II

(The Stitched Book)

A different incarnation of the Book of the Dead, discovered years later. This version contains its own dark writings and unleashed the Deadites surrounding Mia Allen and her friends. This book deals with the entity known as "Shaitan", also known as "The Abomination". It's deadites enjoy body horror and self mutilation and is a vulgar, sick entity filled with hate. It is believed this is the same book from Army Of Darkness that contained the void.

This Necronomicon is connected to the events of Evil Dead 4 (2013).

Necronomicon Book III

(The Teeth Book)

Another ancient copy of the Necronomicon, hidden away until it was discovered beneath Los Angeles. Its pages brought a new generation of Deadites into the world. This book deals with the Marauder entity. It's deadites are obssessed with family bonds and bloodlines, and fully embody thebessence of those they possess, making their victims appear like corrupted versions of their former selves. This book is often nicknamed "Bitey McFly" due its behaviour in Army of Darkness.

This Necronomicon is connected to Evil Dead Rise and future stories within this timeline, such as Evil Dead Burn.
