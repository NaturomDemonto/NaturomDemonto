The Evil Dead Universe

Understanding the Necronomicons

Although the Evil Dead franchise spans multiple films, television series, videogames and comics, the mythology revolves around one central idea... The Necronomicon. Over time, several different Books of the Dead have been discovered. Each possesses its own appearance, passages and dark history, yet all serve the same ancient evil. This archive is designed to explain which stories belong to each Necronomicon and how the Evil Dead universe fits together.
